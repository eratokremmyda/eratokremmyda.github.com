Short Bio

Originally from Athens (Greece), Erato A. Kremmyda is a New York based composer primarily devoted to collaborative work. Currently focusing on theatre, film and documentary, Erato explores collaborations all over the world and loves the challenge of every new project. She is a 2006 Fulbright fellow and a proud member of ASCAP, the Dramatists Guild of America, the American Composers Forum and Bastard Playground. A graduate of Tisch School of the Arts (scholar), Steinhardt NYU, Athens University and Athens Conservatory, she has been awarded for her music both in Greece and in the United States.

[Full resume, references and more work samples available upon request.]

Current/recent collaborations: Maggie-Kate Coleman, Olympia Dukakis, Sam Pinkleton, Onur Karaoglu, Rob Heller, Dimitris Bogdanos, Robin Sandusky, Ioli Andreadi, Jay Stern, Mason Beggs, Ella Rose Chary, Mark Schneider, Jack O’Brien, Maria Gabriella Pezzo, Wenwen Ren.
