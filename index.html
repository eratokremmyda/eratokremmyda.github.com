Resume

Erato A. Kremmyda is a New York City based composer. Born and raised in Athens (Greece), she explores collaborations from all over the world and loves the challenge of every new project. A Fulbright scholar, awarded for her music both in the United States and in Greece, she is a graduate of Tisch School of the Arts (MFA), Steinhardt NYU (MM), the University of Athens and Athens Conservatory. A member of the Dramatists Guild of America, ASCAP and Bastard Playground at the Drama League among a lot of other things, she really doesn't like long bios and would love it if you contacted her personally here.
